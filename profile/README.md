## FAQ

- ### Is it free to use ?

Yes, the website creation part of the project is fully free, if anyone is trying to sell you the app, it is a scam.


The hosting part however is not free, check our billing section on our website for any information you might need.


- ### What is the advantage compared to other solutions ?

NexaWeb aims to offer a simple, effective, ecological and local solution and option to create your website.
Due to european and french security norms for data, we're following the RGPD needs and no data is sold or used with a nefarious intent.

- ### I might need help in the future for the hosting and upkeep parts of the website, is that a service you offer ?

If you are opting for our paid hosting option (and you are right to do so), we are providing different services : 

Claiming the domain name for your website

Handling the hosting part directly

Updating and upkeeping your website if any changes are needed
